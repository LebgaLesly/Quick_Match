QUICK MATCH

At the beginning of the game, all the cards are mixed up and laid in columns and rows face down.
The Player starts and turns over two cards keeping in mind that only 9 trials are available
If the cards do not match (it is not a pair), they turn back over and a trial is taken off .
If the Player is unable to find a match after 9 trials then the game ends.
However, if the two cards match, it is a pair! He keeps the cards and no trial is taken off
When the players finds all the pairs, the game is over and the player wins!
